Hi, I'm Sophia!

I was born and raised in Cincinnati, Ohio, but I have lived in Ann Arbor, Michigan for the past two years attending the University of Michigan. After starting out in Computer Science, I decided to switch to the School of Information to focus more on the intersection of people, systems, and technology. Within Information, I have decided to concetrate on User Experience Design. I am an Experienced Research Assistant interested in web design, accessibility, and how technology can be applied to public health. I'm also a creative writing minor, and I love to read and write fiction and poetry. Check out what I'm currently reading. My three favorite authors include Toni Morrison, Shirley Jackson, and Virginia Woolf. I think a good sentence is a lot like a good design: carefully selected details work together to craft a beautiful whole product.
